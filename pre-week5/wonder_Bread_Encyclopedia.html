History

Wonder Bread was created by The Taggart Baking Company of Indianapolis, Indiana. On May 21, 1921, they began the production of Wonder Bread as we know it today. The name was inspired by a feeling of "wonder" the vice president Elmer Cline felt when he attended a balloon race. He was inspired by all the balloons of varying colors up in the sky, this is also where the iconic logo came from. Then, in the 1930s the bakign company began to advertise Wonder Bread in it's sliced form nationwide. This was revolutionary at the time as it was one of the first bread companies to do this.
