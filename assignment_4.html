Accesibility Statement

When it came to creating the form I found that what I had written wasn’t very accessible to those who would have to use text-to-speech readers or similar needs. It’s basic in how it’s written with little use of alt/messages within the code and the setup. I also feel as though I do not have enough knowledge on choosing colors and imagery to be more accessible too many different ranges of people’s vision as my designs may be hard to view with some vision impairments. To rectify this I would first conduct a lot of research but then get subjects to review my site and products and give feedback for whatever I may be missing or could improve upon.
